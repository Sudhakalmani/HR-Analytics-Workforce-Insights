## Business Insights from HR Data: Attrition, Demographics & Satisfaction
This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce metrics to support data-driven HR and business decisions. The dashboard analyzes employee demographics, attrition trends, and job satisfaction to identify key factors influencing employee retention.
Key insights include overall employee count, attrition rate, active employees, average age, and gender-based attrition analysis. It further breaks down attrition by department, education field, age group, and gender, helping stakeholders understand workforce patterns and risk areas.
Interactive visualizations such as bar charts, pie charts, heatmaps, and filters enable users to explore trends dynamically. This dashboard helps organizations identify high-attrition departments, age groups with higher turnover, and roles with varying job satisfaction levels, supporting strategic workforce planning and retention initiatives.

## Key Features:
- Overall employee metrics: headcount, attrition count, attrition rate, active employees
- Attrition analysis by department, gender, age group, and education field
- Job satisfaction analysis across different job roles
- Interactive filters for dynamic data exploration
- Clear visual storytelling for business and HR stakeholders
